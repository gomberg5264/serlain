Serlain alpha

An unofficial frontend for Internet Archive's Wayback Machine (archive.org/web)!

To start, right-click on this browser's title bar and select the era you want to browse the web in. The sites you visit will then be shown as they appeared back then, as archived by the Wayback Machine.

Note that Serlain is currently in alpha, and will likely either exhibit unwanted behavior or refuse to carry out the desired behavior in at least some cases. You can send bug reports and queries to sw@tarpeeksihyvaesoft.com.
